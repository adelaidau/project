Florence

Overview

Florence is a main city in Italy where you can find many things to do. It is very romantic, perfect to walk around and it is very close to Tuscany where you can go to different small towns which are very unique and all have amazing views, church, museums and amazing food.

Restaurant Recommendations

  • Enoteca Pinchiori: Really good, authentic Italian food. The pasta at the top is from this restaurant and one of the best Pomodoro of my life.
  • Aldovrandi: Best Bistecca alla Florentina, one of the most popular dish in Florence

Amalfi Coast

The Amalfi Coast is one of the most spectacular places I have been to. Separate a couple of days so you can go on a roadtrip through different towns. My suggestion would be to stay in a centric town and then travel throughout the days. You will find unique towns, very nice to walk around and with amazing views.

Restaurant Recommendations

  • San Pietro de Positano: Beautiful hotel and with the best brick oven pizza in the coast
  • Le Sirenuse: Best restaurant for the sunset and very romantic with more elaborated Italian food.
