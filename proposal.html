Travel Blog

My Website will be about my traveling experiences and I will highlight the favorite countries I have visited.

The objective of my website is to highlight the favorite places I have traveled to around the world. I want to show these places through pictures primarly and then give some recommendations for travelers who want to go there.

My target audience will be young people who love to travel all around the world and love to learn about history and new cultures.

I want each page of my website to be about one of my favorite countries. The homepage will have an overview of the countries I have liked the most and then once you click to an image of a specific country it will take you to a page for that specific country with recommendations for restaurants, tourism and other important information I found for someone who is interested in visiting these countries.
