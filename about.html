Travel Souls is a travel blog by Adelaida Urrea. I have always been very passionate for traveling since I was very young. My favorite thing about traveling is that you are able to explore different cultures, which I believe leads to a lot of inspiration and reflection of where you come from and what you want from life. It gives you a new and different perspective through once in a lifetime, unique experiences.

Some of these experiences come in different forms. I love eating in different countries and experiencing different types of flavors, which also reflect the culture. For this reason, before traveling I always research for the best restaurants with very authentic food. I do online research but also ask for recommendations from people who have already visited these places or preferably locals.
